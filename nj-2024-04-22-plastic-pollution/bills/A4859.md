# A4859

**Synopsis:** Eliminates prohibition on paper bags, and allows packaging options, for certain grocery orders for three years; requires program for disposition of reusable bags; extends timeframe for use of certain bags by food banks and pantries.

**Abstract:** Paper bags-eliminate prohibition & allow packaging options, cert grocery orders
