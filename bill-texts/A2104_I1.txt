SYNOPSIS 
 
Requires DEP to prioritize funding for certain projects for acquisition of 
lands for recreation and conservation purposes and certain environmental 
infrastructure projects that include, or allow for, flood mitigation projects.

STATEMENT 
45 
 
46 
 
This bill would direct the Department of Environmental 
47 
Protection (DEP) to provide additional priority points to certain 
48 


=== Page 9 ===
 
A2104 KENNEDY, SPEARMAN 
9 
 
 
applicants that seek funding, for Blue Acres projects and for 
1 
wastewater and drinking water infrastructure projects. 
2 
 
Specifically, under the bill, projects that include, or allow for, a 
3 
flood mitigation project to occur onsite would receive additional 
4 
priority points in the ranking for funding pursuant to the respective 
5 
program.  The DEP would factor these additional points into its 
6 
calculations when establishing:  (1) the list of projects eligible to 
7 
receive “Blue Acres funding”; (2) the “clean water project priority 
8 
list,” pursuant to section 20 of P.L.1985, c.334 (C.58:11B-20); and 
9 
(3) the “drinking water project priority list,” pursuant to section 24 
10 
of P.L.1997, c.224 (C.58:11B-20.1). 
11 
 
As used in the bill, “Blue Acres project” means any project of 
12 
the State or a qualifying tax exempt nonprofit organization to 
13 
acquire, for recreation and conservation purposes, lands that have 
14 
been damaged by, or may be prone to incurring damage caused by, 
15 
storms or storm-related flooding, or that may buffer or protect other 
16 
lands from such damage. 
17
