STATEMENT 
14 
 
15 
 
This bill prohibits State and local government entities that have 
16 
the authority to contract for the purchase of goods and services 
17 
from purchasing single use plastic beverage containers.  The bill 
18 
provides exemptions to any binding contractual obligations for the 
19 
purchase of goods or services entered into prior to the effective date 
20 
of this bill or when the procurement of an alternative to single use 
21 
plastic beverage containers would not be economically feasible. The 
22 
bill directs State agencies and local government entities authorized 
23 
to purchase or contract for goods and services to revise their 
24 
procurement procedures in order to comply with this bill.
