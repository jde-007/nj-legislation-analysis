# S2307

**Synopsis:** Reduces allowed diversion of funds from stormwater, water, and sewer purposes to municipal and county budgets; requires municipalities and counties to notify Division of Local Government Services of diversions.

**Abstract:** Funds from stormwater/water/sewer purposes-reduces allowed diversion
