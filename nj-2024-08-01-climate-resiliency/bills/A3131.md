# A3131

**Synopsis:** Permits business entities and nonprofit organizations to adopt certain responsibilities related to stormwater management basins; provides corporation business tax credit for adoption of stormwater management basins in certain watersheds.

**Abstract:** Adopt a Stormwater Management Basin Act; provides CBT credit for adoption
